Face Recognition Using PCA Algorithm

The principle component analysis is widely used in face recognition for reducing the dimension of face images by extracting the principle features of the images. This work presents the implementation of face recognition based on principle component analysis. The Yale and ORL face databases are used as the training and testing data sets of the recognition. The PCA technique is used to extract the face features and compute the eigenface of images. And I optimize the PCA algorithm with 2-dimensional PCA. Experiments are performed to compare these two methods. The test results indicate that 2-dimensional PCA has higher recognition rate than PCA.
