Tracking Moving Objects Using Kalman Filter

I am planning to do the detection and tracking of moving cars in a traffic video. Firstly, the features of the objects will be extracted to get the centroid of target. Kalman filter will be used to predict and estimate the position of the objects. And I will implement the algorithm in matlab platform. My goal is to correctly detect the targets and successfully track their position.
